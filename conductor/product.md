# Initial Concept

AI Monorepo: A community-driven, platform-agnostic collection of AI skills, themes, and prompts. This repository is organized as a monorepo of standalone, versioned packages designed to be usable by any general agent (Gemini, Cursor, Claude, Copilot, etc.).

# Product Guide

## Vision
To create the industry-standard repository for modular, agentic capabilities that work seamlessly across all major AI interfaces (Gemini, Cursor, Claude, etc.). We aim to empower both developers and users by providing a curated, high-quality, and platform-agnostic collection of "superpowers" for their AI agents.

## Core Pillars
- **Platform Agnosticism:** Every package must be designed to work across multiple AI platforms without platform-specific locking.
- **Modularity:** Skills, themes, and prompts are standalone units that can be versioned and used independently.
- **Security & Safety:** Emphasizing human-in-the-loop execution and isolated environments (DevContainers).
- **Quality & Portability:** Standardized structures and POSIX-compliant scripts to ensure broad compatibility.

## Target Audience
- **Agent Developers:** Looking for pre-built, tested interactive capabilities and prompts.
- **AI Enthusiasts:** Seeking to customize their AI experience with themes and specialized tools.
- **Community Contributors:** Who want to share their own AI skills and workflows in a structured way.

## Key Feature Categories
1. **Interactive Skills:** Capabilities that allow agents to interact with the real world (git, search, system operations).
2. **UI/UX Themes:** Enhancements for AI interfaces like Gemini CLI, providing better visual feedback and accessibility.
3. **Prompt Collections:** Versioned, specialized prompt templates for common and complex tasks.
4. **Shared Utilities:** Tools and wrappers that simplify the creation and use of AI-driven workflows.
